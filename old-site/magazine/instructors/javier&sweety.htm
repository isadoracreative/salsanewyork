Javier & Sweety

 

 SalsaNewYork Listed Instructors:
 Javier & Sweety  are New Jersey based mambo instructors and performers who have been in our scene for many years and who have a good following of students and other dancers who attend their events.  They run their own dance studio, have an adult and children's performing group, have a monthly social and, in addition, Javier is DJ who plays at various NJ events.  Click Here to read about them.

Schedule

Javier & Sweety are long-time mambo instructors in New Jersey.  They teach their own classes and also teach classes for several large corporations.  They teach all levels of mambo to adults and kids and also Ladies Styling.
