Joe Burgos
of
Piel Canela Dance

 

 


 SalsaNewYork Listed Instructor:
 Joe Burgos has been a New York City based On 2 mambo instructor for many years and is the director of the Piel Canela Dance Studio & the Piel Canela Dance Company.  They offer dance classes, performing groups and dance theater shows throughout the year.
