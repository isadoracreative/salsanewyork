    Price of Classes
    All classes are $12 per person per class or try combining Ladies Styling or Shine Crazy with anyone of our classes on Saturday for a total of $20!

    That's 2 1/2 hrs for $20!!

    Any student that has taken at least one group class is eligible for private classes at a rate of $40 per hour, take it with a friend and it totals $50; that's $25 each a great value!!

    Any one not taking group classes please call or email us for information.

    Here's some more value!! Once you have passed to the next level of classes all classes at a lower level are free for you to take. That is, if you're an Intermediate I student then you may take Beginner II classes for free, but only if you pay for your regular Intermediate class. So warm up in a level lower and heat it up in yours!
