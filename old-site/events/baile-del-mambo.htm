BAILE DEL MAMBO @ STEPPING OUT STUDIOS

DATE OF EVENT Held on the 2nd Friday of each month.
TIME Normal salsa social dancing hours are 10:15 PM to 3:00 AM.  Salsa class at 9:30 PM by guest instructor, bachata class 10:15 PM in separate room.  If times are different, they will be noted on our Main Calendar or on the flyer above.

LOCATION

 Held at Stepping Out Studios, 37 West 26th Street (between 6th Avenue & Broadway), 9th floor, Manhattan, NYC.  Phone 646-742-9400.
VIEW LOCATION MAP Click Here For Map.  Travel instructions are below.
PRESENTED BY DJ John John .
CONTACT INFO

DJ John John - 718-290-3400  -  FaceBook Page .

FaceBook Page for this event.  Web Page for this event.
TYPE OF EVENT & VENUE This is a classic On 2 mambo social held in a very large dance studio with a professional wooden ballroom floor, a pro sound system, seating and an area for refreshments.
 
MUSIC Mostly pure classic salsa plus a little cha cha, bachata & merengue.
DANCE PERFORMANCES Sometimes. They would be listed on our Main Calendar.
DJ DJ John John and invited guest DJs plus live Afro-Cuban percussion.
BAND No.
DANCE FLOOR Large nicely finished wooden ballroom dance floor which is "medium speed.
PRICES Regular admission is $15.  Admission includes classes for salsa, bachata & kizomba.  For special events, prices may be a few dollars higher.
BEVERAGES AND FOOD Refreshments are available for purchase and there are complementary snacks, hot food & bottled water.
DRESS CODE Casual

DANCE TIMING

Almost entirely "New York On 2" timing and style.
DESCRIPTION

DJ John John brings you this classic salsa event once per month.  You can link to it on Facebook at https://www.facebook.com/events/1589937541307864/  for more info.
 
HOW TO GET THERE The event is being held at the Stepping Out Studios, 37 West 26th Street (between Broadway and 6th Avenue/aka Avenue of the Americas).   For exact location on a map which you can zoom in and out, Click Here For Map.  Closest subway stations are the 23rd or 28th Street Stations on the N, R or W lines, and also the 23rd Street Station on the F or V lines.  It is usually fairly easy to park within a few blocks if you drive around a few minutes.

For more information on public transportation, click on NYC Subway-Bus-Train Maps , or NY-NJ PATH Train Maps or Long Island Railroad Maps or MetroNorth Railroad Maps .
 
OTHER INFORMATION

Dance classes included with admission:  salsa, bachata, kizomba.
