YAMULEE'S BOOGIE DOWN MAMBO PARTY

 

DATE OF EVENT Usually once a month, sometimes twice, usually on a Friday or a Saturday.  See our Main Calendar and above flyers for exact dates.
TIME Usually 10:00 PM to 2:30 AM or later.  See Main Calendar for exact times on a particular night. 

LOCATION

 The Yamulee Dance Studio is located at 2435 Grand Concourse (just south of the corner of E. 188th St., 1 block south of Fordham Rd.), 2nd floor, in the Bronx, NY, 10468, in the Fordham Heights neighborhood.  It is on the 2nd floor over the Sneaker Plus store.  Studio phone 718-292-9768.
VIEW LOCATION MAP Click Here For Map .  Travel instructions are below.  See map for parking below.
PRESENTED BY Instructor and performer Osmar Perrones, Founder and Director of the Yamulee Dance Studio.
CONTACT INFO For information call the studio at 718-292-9768, or Osmar at 917-597-3576 and email Osmar@yamulee.com .  See their web site at www.Yamulee.com and their FaceBook Page for this event.
TYPE OF EVENT & VENUE This is a very enjoyable "hard-core uptown" salsa social held in a very large newly renovated dance studio which attracts dancers of all levels, including some very advanced ones & instructors/performers, from NY, CT and NJ.   The excellent music is a wide selection of different styles and eras of very danceable salsa.  There is definitely an "uptown" Latino atmosphere here representing the authentic flavor and roots of our New York style On 2 mambo.  And the MC is the one and only famous Levi "La Lena". 
 
MUSIC Almost entirely pure classic salsa plus a little cha cha, bachata & merengue.
DANCE PERFORMANCES Almost always, usually local and out-of-town mambo teams perform. They are listed on our Main Calendar.
DJ Usually music is DJd by Yamulee's resident DJs,  DJ Osmar Perrones and DJ "Doc Salsa" Steve Shaw (from www.SalsaNewYork.com),  DJ Amauri Guillen , DJ Jose "La Salsa" and DJ Henry "Fire".  See our Main Calendar for DJs on any particular date.
BAND None.
DANCE FLOOR Very large new wooden studio dance floor which is generally "medium speed".  However, when the weather and the dancing gets really hot, the floor can become "a bit slower".  So wear your "medium" to "more slippery" shoes.
PRICES Regular admission is $12 - $15.  Sometimes it is a few dollars more, based on special features of the social for that night.
BEVERAGES AND FOOD Beverages for sale such as water, Gatorade, etc. 
DRESS CODE Let's call it "street hot or classy casual".  Everyone is free to come as they are, but many people dress hot or flashy or sexy in a casual street style.  Above all, come to dance hard to the excellent music.

DANCE TIMING

Entirely "New York On 2" timing and style.
DESCRIPTION Presented by Osmar Perrones and the staff of the Yamulee Dance Studio and Dance Company at their own newly renovated big facility, this Yamulee Social has been running since 2003.  They invite all mambo dancers to come and enjoy the dance performances by invited groups and lots of classic salsa by the resident DJs on their professional sound system.  The music mix is one of the best and most versatile of any mambo social in NYC, spanning the broad range of salsa styles that are actually heard in the NYC Latino communities, and also including some occasional well-chosen bachata & merengue.  And the dancers range from basic beginners to some of the best in New York City, including many Latinos.  And the MC is the one and only famous Levi "La Lena".  No age limits.  See our Calendar for the list of performing dance teams and DJs. 

The very large dance floor is wood and it is about "medium speed" so wear appropriate shoes.  Beverages will be available for sale.  The location of the studio is right next to the nearby train station in a residential/commercial neighborhood in the Fordham Heights section of the Bronx, a convenient trip on the D train express.

Click on Pictures of the Yamulee Social to see photographs of this event by Amir Shimoni on his web site www.MamboPics.com .
 
HOW TO GET THERE The Yamulee Dance Studio is located at 2435 Grand Concourse (just south of the corner of E. 188th St. and 1block south of Fordham Rd.), 2nd floor, in the Bronx, NY, 10468, in the Fordham Heights neighborhood.  It is on the 2nd floor over the Sneaker Plus clothing store.  Studio phone 718-292-9768.  Click Here For Map .  See map for parking below.

Nearby subways & buses:  Closest trains are the D & B trains to the Fordham Road Station.  The station exit at the south end of the train is right on the corner of Yamulee, so be near the back of the uptown D or B train.

A few blocks farther is the #4 train to the Fordham Road Station at Jerome Avenue.  For buses, the Bx1, Bx2, Bx12 & BxM4 all stop at Fordham Rd. & Grand Concourse.

By Car:  From the Cross Bronx Expressway, take the Jerome Avenue exit and head north on Jerome Ave.  Turn right onto 176th Street and then turn left onto the Grand Concourse.  Then go north to E. 188th Street which is 1 block south of Fordham Road.  The studio is on the southwest corner of Grand Concourse and E. 188th Street, over the Sneaker Plus clothing store. 

From northern Manhattan (Washington Heights, Inwood) - Take Broadway north, then turn right onto 207th Street and continue across the bridge into the Bronx.  207th St. becomes Fordham Road.  Take Fordham Road to the Grand Concourse, turn right and go 1 block to just below E. 188th Street.  The Yamulee Studio is on your right, over a store.  The BxM4 bus goes along West 207th Street and crosses the bridge to the Bronx and runs along Fordham Road to Grand Concourse.

Parking:  There is easy street parking in the neighborhood on the Grand Concourse & side streets.  There are also several nearby parking garages & lots at a reasonable cost for several hours, usually $8 - $10.  See map of parking garages below.

For more information on public transportation, click on  NYC Subway-Bus-Train Maps , or NY-NJ PATH Train Maps or Long Island Railroad Maps or MetroNorth Railroad Maps .
 
OTHER INFORMATION See Osmar Perrones' SalsaNewYork Instructor Listing and also the performers page of the Yamulee Dance Company .  There web site is www.Yamulee.com .  They sell their own DVD instructional DVDs and also dance shoes.
 

Yamulee Nearby Parking Garages & Lots

Note:  There are 3 parking garages open 24/7 on E. 187th St. (between Valentine Ave. & Grand Concourse).
The one closest to Valentine Ave. on your right side is the cheapest.
"Manhattan Parking Garage" (below) on E. 188th St. (between Valentine & Tiebout) is closed.
There's an outdoor parking lot on Valentine on your left between 187th & 188th Streets.
Farther away, there's an indoor parking garage on Jerome between E. Fordham & E. 190th St.
Typical price for several hours at night is $8 - $10 (plus tip).
