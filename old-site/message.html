The current long-time owners/hosts of SalsaNewYork.com, Steve Shaw & Manny Siverio, are retiring, but the site will continue.  SalsaNewYork has covered the NYC scene for the On 2 dancers for 20 years, non-stop.  For me, it was a great pleasure to help you salsa lovers find where to go dancing, and it came out of my passion for classic salsa music and dancing On 2.  For those of you who used SalsaNewYork these many years, we thank you; you made our efforts worthwhile.  But all things must come to an end some day and, for us, it is time to retire, though with many regrets. 

 However, we have happily chosen Marlon "International" to take over.   See his web site at https://www.marlonintl.com/ and his Facebook page at https://www.facebook.com/SalsaNYC .  He has been in our local NYC On 2 scene for some 17 years in many roles & knows most events & people.  We ask your patience while he develops a new modern platform to work on all devices, then we fill the site with the many dancing events.  Meanwhile, the June 2018 calendar is up to date. 

Steve Shaw, aka DJ "DocSalsa".
