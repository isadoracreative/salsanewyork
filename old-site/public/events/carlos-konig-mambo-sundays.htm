MAMBO SUNDAYS PARTY WITH CARLOS KONIG

    

DATE OF EVENT Held on the 2nd and 4th Sunday of every month.
TIME 5:30 - 9:30 PM.

LOCATION

 The social is being held at Ripley-Grier Studios, 520 8th Avenue (between 36th & 37th Streets), 17th floor, midtown Manhattan, NYC. 
VIEW LOCATION MAP Click Here For Map . (travel instructions below)
PRESENTED BY Instructor and performer Carlos Konig.
CONTACT INFO Email - CarlosKonig@hotmail.com .  Web site - www.CarlosKonig.com .
TYPE OF EVENT & VENUE This is a salsa dance social held in a good size recently renovated dance studio and there are 2 extra studio rooms available when the crowd gets bigger.  The wooden dance floor is excellent, "medium speed", and it never gets sticky even in summers when with people dancing on it.
MUSIC Almost entirely pure classic salsa plus a little cha cha.
DANCE PERFORMANCES Very occasionally. They would be listed on our Main Calendar.
DJ Carlos Konig is the DJ and also there are sometimes guest DJs.
BAND None
DANCE FLOOR Excellent wooden dance floor which is "medium speed".
PRICES Starting November 2015, admission will be $13.
BEVERAGES AND FOOD Beverages are sold.  Various snacks like chips, pretzels, muffins are free.
DRESS CODE Very casual.  Come as you like.

DANCE TIMING

Entirely "New York On 2" timing and style.
DESCRIPTION Presented by instructor,  performer, choreographer, DJ and musician Carlos Konig, this social will feature lots of classic salsa music, with an occasional cha cha, by DJ Carlos Konig and other guest DJ's.  That means you'll be hearing some very good dancing music.  There will be dance shows from time to time which will be listed on our Calendar.  Held in a brand new studio, the wooden dance floor is "medium speed", so it is suitable for most shoes.  There are two dancing rooms available, plus another one ready to open if it gets crowded, as well as 2 other rooms for relaxing & socializing.  Refreshments are available for reasonable prices along with free snacks such as chips, pretzels & muffins.   Carlos is well-known to many of us as the smiling salsero with some of the fastest feet you've ever seen.  He is gracious and a true gentleman.  His classes & events are always friendly and the ladies tell me that dancing with him is a very graceful experience.  He invites everyone to come and join him at his social, a place for great dancing and friendly socializing.  The wooden dance floor is excellent, "medium speed", and it never gets sticky even in summers when with people dancing on it.

Click on Carlos Konig Social Pictures to see photographs of this event by Amir Shimoni on his web site www.MamboPics.com .
 
HOW TO GET THERE

The social is being held at Ripley-Grier Studios, 520 8th Avenue (between 36th & 37th Streets), 17th floor, midtown Manhattan, NYC.  To see a Google map, which can be zoomed in and out, click on Click Here For Map.  Closest trains are A, C, E and Long Island Railroad to 34th Street station.  A couple of blocks away is the 34th Street station for the 1, 2, 3 and LIRR trains (on 7th Avenue).  The 8th and 7th Avenue buses also stop very nearby.  Street parking is possible but you have to search for a while.

For more information on public transportation, click on  NYC Subway-Bus-Train Maps , or NY-NJ PATH Train Maps or Long Island Railroad Maps or MetroNorth Railroad Maps .

OTHER INFORMATION

Carlos invites everyone from the local and out-of-town mambo community to come and join his party.  Carlos is a long time NYC salsa instructor offering classes & private lessons.  Email - CarlosKonig@hotmail.com .  Web site - www.CarlosKonig.com .
