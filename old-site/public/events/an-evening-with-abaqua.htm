AN EVENING WITH ABAKUA

Full Information Below This Flyer


DATE OF EVENT Held on the 4th Friday of each month.
TIME Usually 10:00 PM to 2:00 AM.  From 9:30 - 10:00 PM there is a boogaloo warm-up with an instructor plus music playing.  If there are time changes, they will be noted on our Main Calendar.

LOCATION

 This event is held at Club 412 which is part of the You Should Be Dancing Studio, 412 Eighth Avenue (between 30th & 31st Streets), 4th floor, on the west side of midtown Manhattan, near Madison Square Garden.  Dial 007 for to get in the building's front door on street level.
VIEW LOCATION MAP Click Here For Map . (travel instructions below)
PRESENTED BY This salsa social, hosted by Manny Blackett of the Abakua Latin Dance Company, is presented by instructor and performer Frankie Martinez of Abakua Afro-Latin Dancers.
CONTACT INFO Manny Blackett - Email:  Emanuel.Blackett@gmail.com

You Should Be Dancing Studio - Telephone: 212-244-0011.  FAX: 212-868-3281.  Email: info@youshouldbedancing.net .  Web site:  www.YouShouldBeDancing.net .  Business hours begin at 12:00 Noon.

For information about Frankie Martinez and Abakua Afro-Latin Dancers, Click Here.
TYPE OF EVENT & VENUE Held at the newly renovated and very charming Club 412 , which is set up like a club with soft lighting, seating, attractive decor, a cash bar and a good DJ sound system.
MUSIC Almost entirely pure classic old school salsa plus a good amount of cha cha.
DANCE PERFORMANCES Very occasionally. They would be listed on our Main Calendar.
DJ DJ Frankie Martinez and occasional guest DJs.
BAND None.
DANCE FLOOR The all-wooden floor ranges from "medium to a bit fast speed".
PRICES Reasonable and typical admission price for a local salsa social.
BEVERAGES AND FOOD There is a regular bar with both alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages.  Often there are drink specials, particularly before 11:00 PM.
DRESS CODE Casual.

DANCE TIMING

Almost entirely "New York On 2" timing and style.
DESCRIPTION A night of classic salsa and cha cha dance music played by DJ Frankie Martinez.  This salsa social, hosted by Manny Blackett of the Abakua Latin Dance Company, is presented by instructor and performer Frankie Martinez of Abakua Afro-Latin Dancers, and is held at the newly renovated and very charming Club 412 , which is set up like a club with soft lighting, seating, attractive decor, a real cash bar and a good DJ sound system.   This is an opportunity to dance in an attractive studio setting with lots of dance floor space and a very friendly environment.   There is a very reasonable admission price and beverages will be available for purchase.  The dress code is "casual" and all ages are welcome and invited. 

Click on Pictures of Abakua Social to see photographs of this event by Amir Shimoni on his web site www.MamboPics.com .
 
HOW TO GET THERE This event is held at Club 412 which is part of the You Should Be Dancing Studio, 412 Eighth Avenue (between 30th & 31st Streets), 4th floor, on the west side of midtown Manhattan, near Madison Square Garden and Penn Station.  It can be reached by many trains:  A, C, E, 1, 2, 3 and the Long Island Railroad LIRR.  All 7th, 8th and 9th Avenue, and 34th Street crosstown, busses stop at 34th Street.

For more information on public transportation, click on  NYC Subway-Bus-Train Maps , or NY-NJ PATH Train Maps or Long Island Railroad Maps or MetroNorth Railroad Maps .
 
OTHER INFORMATION Frankie Martinez and members of his Abakua Afro-Latin Dancers teach salsa classes and privates at the studios of You Should Be Dancing Studio, which also offers classes and private lessons in many different types of dance taught by a variety of different instructors.
