Smiling David

Broadway Dance
School

 SalsaNewYork Listed Instructor:
Currently Smiling David is an instructor and performer in Newark, NJ, though he is frequently seen at mambo events around the City.  And it is true:  He smiles a lot!  He was formerly a member of the professional team of the Karisma Dance Company and now heads up his own studio for several years and is the choreographer and director of his Pzazz Dance Company which has performed internationally.  - Manny Siverio
