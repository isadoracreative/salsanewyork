 SalsaNewYork Listed Instructor:
 Shani Talmor has been a NYC On 2 instructor for a number of years.  Trained since childhood in ballet, jazz, hip hop, samba, Afro-Cuban & mambo On 2, she has lived, instructed & performed in Israel, Spain, Italy & New York.  Locally, she has performed with the Caribbean Soul Dance Company, Eddie Torres, Sekou McMiller & others.  She is also the choreographer & director of the mambo team Shani's Girls.  She currently teaches classes in all levels in midtown Manhattan, including a special 8 week salsa bootcamp for beginners.  - Manny Siverio
