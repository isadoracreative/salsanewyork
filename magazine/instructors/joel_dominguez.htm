Joel Dominguez

 

 

 

 

 SalsaNewYork Listed Instructor:
Joel Dominguez  has been an On 2 instructor for many years, beginning in his native Mexico, then while living in Texas and now here in New York City.  He is an active member of our On 2 mambo community as a teacher, performer and promoter.  He has performed and taught at various international salsa congresses and has competed in a number of contests including winning the Texas state competition to represent that state at the Puerto Rican Salsa Congress.  He also co-ran the Salsa Passion Dance Company.  Click Here to see his complete salsa bio.  He currently runs his own salsa school which features a special "Salsa Bootcamp" for learning salsa in 8 weeks.  Click Here to see videos of Joel teaching, social dancing & performing.   -  Steve Shaw.
