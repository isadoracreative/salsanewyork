Marcus Gonzalez

 SalsaNewYork Listed Instructor:

Marcus Gonzalez is an approved mambo SalsaNewYork instructor.  His training included instruction with Carlos Vazquez of La Salsa De Hoy Dance Studio, Ray Colon of Salsa Salsa Dance Studio and the Mambo King Eddie Torres of the Eddie Torres Dance Studio.  Marcus has performed with Tropical Image Dancers and the Latin Fever Dance Company.  He is currently co-owner of the Salsa Salsa Dance Studios in Brooklyn, NY.  Click on Marcus Gonzalez's Complete Bio.
