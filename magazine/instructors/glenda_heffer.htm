Glenda Heffer

SalsaNewYork Listed Instructor:
 Veteran New York City On 2 instructor and performer "La Mambera" Glenda Heffer has performed & taught nationally and internationally with both the "Eddie Torres Dancers" and later in the team "Mambo D & La Mambera".  She has been in the On 2 New York salsa scene for close to 20 years. She has worked with some of the biggest names in mambo to share her love of dancing with everyone she meets both domestically and abroad. Manny Siverio
