Winsome Lee Sanchez

 

 SalsaNewYork Listed Instructor:  Winsome Lee Sanchez
Winsome Lee is also known as Winsome Lee Sanchez, named that by David Melendez to make her an honorary Latina because of her dedication to Latin dance, music and culture.  She is one of New York City's most experienced On 2 mambo personalities, as a dancer, instructor, performer and choreographer.  Coming from a strong music and dance background starting very young, she has brought her own unique style, personality and energy to her work as an instructor, performer and choreographer.  Click Here to see her complete bio and dance experience. - Manny Siverio & Steve Shaw.
