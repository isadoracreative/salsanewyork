SalsaNewYorks Best New Dance Studio Pick
Now-a-days Instructors seem to be a dime a dozen. Six months of lessons and people open up a dance class. Yet it takes more than opening up a dance class to be a successful instructor. One has to be talented, knowledgeable, professional, responsible, creative, nurturing and be both a good motivator and a good communicator. Over the last year The Jai & Candy Dance Studio has managed to break away from the rest of the pack. The studio run by the talented young couple of Jai Catalano and Candy Mena have made a strong impression in the local NY/NJ dance scene. In just a short amount of time that the couple has been teaching; theyve developed a loyal following of students, performed at various Salsa Congresses both here and abroad, launched their own website, started their own dance company and have performed at various history making events such as the Off Broadway Mambo Musical LATIN MADNESS. Its for this reason that SalsaNewYork Magazine has given The Jai & Candy Dance Studio the title of BEST NEW DANCE STUDIO IN NJ.
