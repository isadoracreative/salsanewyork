Cynthia La China

 

 

 

 SalsaNewYork Listed Instructor:
Currently Cynthia La China has been a NYC metropolitan area salsa instructor for a number of years, teaching in both studios and clubs.  In addition, she has performed with several local teams such as Revelation Dancers and the Rhythm Divas, and she is the director and choreographer of her own teams Soy Caliente Dance Project and the NY Salsa Baby Dance Team.  Over the years, she has trained with several of the major dance studios in New York City. - Manny Siverio
