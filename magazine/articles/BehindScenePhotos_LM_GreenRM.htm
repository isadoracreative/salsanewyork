Behind the Scene Photos of
Latin Madness IV 

  • The Green Room: 
    The Green Room a.k.a. The Latin Madness War Room. Its our pre-show meeting place. Jami gives everyone notes pertaining to the previous night's show. Her notes can cover everything from mis-steps and styling notes to missed cues and formation corrections. Cast members can be seen stretching, warming up or just listening. After notes are given, the cast gets together for a group prayer. Then its show time.
