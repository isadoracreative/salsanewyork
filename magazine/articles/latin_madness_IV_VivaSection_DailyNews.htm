Theatre
LATIN MADNESS IV

An Exciting Locura Latina

Latin Madness could very well be the future of musical theater. By combining hot, pulsating Latin Beats with fast stepping, gyrating bodies in constant motion and cutting edge humor, Latin Madness could lead the way to a new brand of entertainment. Fresh from Los Angeles and England , Latin Madness will be playing for a limited run in New York prior to returning to Europe early next year.

Latin Madness is the story of two regular guys from the Bronx who are trying to bring their show to Broadway. Join our tour guides, actors Herb Quinones and Rich Ramirez as they take you on this captivating trip down memory lane as we go to the Palladium with a wonderful tribute to the Big Three, Machito, Tito Rodriguez and Tito Puente. The Latin Madness Orchestra is under the direction of Jose Madera former director of the Tito Puente Orchestra.  

The 25-member Latin Madness dancers are international in scope and as versatile as they are good, whether dancing mambo, Cha-Cha-Cha. Merengue or Hip-Hop. They have toured England , Italy , France , Japan , Israel , Turkey , Holland , Toronto , Switzerland , Puerto Rico , Columbia , Belgium and Peru . Also, Los Angeles , Chicago , Miami , Houston , Atlanta , Washington D.C. and many other cities.

By all means go see Latin Madness.

WHEN: October 16  November 2.

WHERE: Hostos Center for Arts and Culture, 450 Grand Concourse, Bronx .


Tickets now on Sale . For Information visit www.latin-madness.com. For tickets and information call (718) 518-4455 or (718) 595-1000.
