Compiled -by Manny Siverio

 

LATIN MADNESS IV STORYLINE
THE CRITICALLY ACCLAIMED OFF-BROADWAY MUSICAL 
RETURNS FOR A FORTH SEASON.

This Double-Dose of Comedy and Dance is a Unique Journey Through The History of Latin Dance in America.

Welcome to the future of musical theater! Latin Madness, LLC brings to the city of New York a new breath of air in the theatrical market. By combining hot pulsating Latin Beats with fast stepping, gyrating bodies in constant motion and cutting edge humor, Latin Madness LLC will lead the way for a new brand of entertainment, providing the same quality results, as a typical Broadway show. Fresh from its limited engagements in Los Angeles and London, Latin Madness will be playing for a limited run in New York prior to returning to Europe early next year. LATIN MADNESS is an exciting blend of comedy and dance that will have you out of your seat and either in the aisles dancing or on the floor in stitches from theuproarious comedy presented on stage.

The story is that of two regular guys from the Bronx who are trying to bring their show to Broadway. Join our tour guides, actors Herb Quiñones and Rich Ramirez as they take you on this captivating trip, which will leave you begging for more. Don't miss the
trip down memory lane as we go to the Palladium with a wonderful tribute to
the Big 3, Machito, Tito Rodriguez and Tito Puente. The Latin Madness Orchestra is under the direction of Jose Madera former director of the Tito
Puente Orchestra.

The twenty five member LATIN MADNESS dance troupe is comprised of an international array of dancers that continue to be on the cutting edge in the world of Latin Dance. These performers are as versatile as they are good. Whether dancing Mambo, Cha-Cha, Merengue or Hip-Hop they take the hot pulsating Latin beats and combine them with their fast stepping, gyrating bodies for a dance extravaganza the likes of which will have you asking a for a Mojito to cool off.

These performers have toured internationally in countries such as England, Italy, France, Japan, Israel, Turkey, Holland, Toronto, Switzerland, Puerto Rico, Colombia, Belgium and Peru. In the United States they have toured cities such as Los Angeles, Chicago, Miami, Houston, Atlanta, Washington D.C. and the list goes on. Come see this original production! Live Music, Live Dancing and Live Comedy! Find out if two guys from the Bronx can make it to Broadway?

Latin Madness Press Release:
Bronx, NY -(FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE)- Latin Madness LLC is leading the way to a new brand of entertainment, providing the same quality results, as a typical Broadway show.  Fresh from its limited sold out engagements in Los Angeles and London, with SRO audiences of 800 and 2,000, repectively, LATIN MADNESS opened last week in the Bronx and has already sold out performances for the two remaining Saturdays in its limited run here in New York prior to returning to Europe early next year.

 LATIN MADNESS is an exciting blend of comedy and dance that will have you out of your seat and either in the aisles dancing or on the floor in stitches from the uproarious comedy presented on stage.  The story is that of two regular guys from the Bronx who are trying to bring their show to Broadway.  Join our tour guides, actors Herb Quiñones and Rich Ramirez as they take you on this captivating trip, which will leave you begging for more.  Dont miss the trip down memory lane as we go to the Palladium with a wonderful tribute to the Big 3, Machito, Tito Rodriguez and Tito Puente.  The LATIN MADNESS Orchestra is under the direction of Jose Madera former director of the Tito Puente Orchestra.

The twenty five member LATIN MADNESS dance troupe is comprised of an international array of dancers that continue to be on the cutting edge in the world of Latin Dance.  These performers are as versatile as they are good.  Whether dancing Mambo, Cha-Cha, Merengue or Hip-Hop they take the hot pulsating Latin beats and combine them with their fast stepping, gyrating bodies for a dance extravaganza the likes of which will have you asking a for a Mojito to cool off.  These performers have toured internationally in countries such as England, Italy, France, Japan, Israel, Turkey, Holland, Toronto, Switzerland, Puerto Rico, Colombia, Belgium and Peru. In the United States they have toured cities such as Los Angeles, Chicago, Miami, Houston, Atlanta, Washington D.C. and the list goes on.

Come see this original production!  Live Music, Live Dancing and Live Comedy!  Find out if two guys from the Bronx can bring their Latino show to Broadway?  Dont miss the excitement as Latin Madness runs from October 16th through November 2nd at the elegant Hostos Center for Arts and Culture. Tickets Now On Sale!  Advance ticket prices are $45 and $40 respectively.
