Behind the Scene Photos of
Latin Madness IV 

  • The Hallway Backstage:
    The area backstage is surrounded by a series of Hallways that lead the cast to Men's dressing rooms, Ladies dressing rooms, make-up rooms, bathrooms, Green Room and the band performing area. During the show the area riddled with performers running back and forth as they perform or run back to get changed. Sometimes there is so little time between numbers that performers have no choice but to get changed directly backstage. I think that no number characterizes this more than Love For Sale. Four of the performers (Nelson, Sherwin, Steve and Manny) have just finished the Cuban Fantasy Fosse-like number and have to rush backstage to stripe down and change. While comedians Herbie and Rich entertain the audience, a support team assists the "men" in putting on wigs, dresses, make-up and shoes.
