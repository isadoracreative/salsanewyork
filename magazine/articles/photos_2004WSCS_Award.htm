SalsaNewYork
is Honored at 6th Annual
West Coast Salsa Congress
(L-R) Albert Torres, Manny Siverio & Steve Shaw.
Photos by David Melendez

This past May 2004, Albert Torres and his West Coast Salsa Congress honored web authors and Co-Hosts of SalsaNewYork Manny Siverio and Steve Shaw for their "dedication to promoting Latin Music and Dance". The authors were both very appreciative of Albert Torres' recognition of their efforts. They would also like to thank Albert for being such a gracious host and for his hospitality while they were L.A.

Scroll down below for several more photos taken during the award ceremony. The photos were provided by our little known shutterbug David Melendez. It seems that David is a man of various skills. Not only is he the promoter to the 2004 NY Salsa Congress (August 19-22), but budding photojournalist.
