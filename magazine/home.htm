 May 2018  -  The SalsaNewYork.com web site is being discontinued by the current owners/hosts, Steve Shaw & Manny Siverio.  At first, we ran into major technical difficulties last month, but even after some partial solutions were found, we realized the time has come for us to finish our work.  SalsaNewYork has covered the NYC scene for the On 2 dancers for 20 years, non-stop.  Especially for me, running the calendar, it has been a lot of work to list an increasing number of events, but it was a great pleasure to help you salsa lovers find where to go dancing, and it came out of my passion for classic salsa music and dancing On 2.  For those of you who used SalsaNewYork these many years, we thank you; you made our efforts worthwhile.  But all things must come to an end some day and, for us, it is time to retire, though with many regrets. 

 

 However, after many people generously offered to continue the site, we have chosen Marlon "International" to take over.   See his web site at https://www.marlonintl.com/ and his Facebook page at https://www.facebook.com/SalsaNYC ,  He has been in our local NYC On 2 scene for some 17 years in many roles & knows most events & people, We ask your patience while he develops a new modern platform to work on all devices, then fills the site the many dancing events.  Thank you.   

For everyone else, as we always say: See you on the dance floor.  

Steve Shaw, aka DJ Doc Salsa,

The Directory is a comprehensive list of "On 2" Mambo dance companies who have performed locally, around the country and around the world.
