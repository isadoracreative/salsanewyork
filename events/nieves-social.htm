NIEVES LATIN DANCE STUDIO SALSA SOCIALS

DATE OF EVENT The Brooklyn Socials are held every Friday.  The Bronx Socials are held on various dates, usually a Saturday.  See our Main Calendar for exact dates.
TIME Usually 9:00 PM to 12:30 AM.

LOCATION

 Brooklyn Social - Held every Friday at the Nieves Latin Dance Studio, 670 Grand Street (corner of Manhattan Ave.), in the Williamsburg neighborhood of Brooklyn, NY  11211.  Studio phone  347-721-3178.

Bronx Social - Held on varying dates at the Nieves Latin Dance Studio, 1215 Morris Avenue (near East 168th Street), in the Morrisania-Concourse-South Bronx neighborhood of the Bronx, NY  10456.  Studio phone 718-590-9000.  See our Main Calendar for exact dates.
VIEW LOCATION MAP Brooklyn Social  -  Click Here For Map.  Travel instructions are below.

Bronx Social  -  Click Here For Map.  Travel instructions are below.
PRESENTED BY Wil Nieves, Founder and Director of the Nieves Latin Dance Studio.
CONTACT INFO Wil Nieves - Web site www.NievesDanceStudio.com , phone 347-721-3178, email NievesDanceStudio@gmail.com .
TYPE OF EVENT & VENUE These are pure On 2 mambo socials held in a good size professional dance studio with approximately 1000 square foot wooden dance floor which is "medium speed", mirrored walls and professional sound system.
MUSIC Almost entirely pure classic salsa plus a little cha cha, bachata.  Sometimes there are drummers.
DANCE PERFORMANCES Occasionally. They would be listed on our Main Calendar.
DJ DJ Wil Nieves and occasionally invited guest DJs.
BAND None.
DANCE FLOOR One 1000 square foot professional wooden dance floor which is "medium speed".
PRICES Usually $5.  There is a discount if you bring a friend or if you are in college or high school.
BEVERAGES AND FOOD Cash water & other beverages are available.
DRESS CODE Casual

DANCE TIMING

"New York On 2" timing and style.
DESCRIPTION These are classic On 2 mambo socials held in the fully equipped dance space of the Nieves Latin Dance Studio in Williamsburg, Brooklyn, and in the South Bronx neighborhood of the Bronx, NYC.  Wil Nieves is the founder, director, instructor and choreographer for the dance performance teams associated with the studio.  He invites all the mambo dancers to come and enjoy their regular weekly Friday social, dancing to mostly classic salsa music plus a bit of bachata and more.  The dancers there are friendly and welcoming to newcomers and there are plenty of partners available.
 
HOW TO GET THERE

The Brooklyn Social  -  This salsa social is held at the Nieves Latin Dance Studio, 670 Grand Street (corner of Manhattan Ave.), in the Williamsburg neighborhood of Brooklyn, NY  11211.  Studio phone  347-721-3178.  Click Here For Map

How to get there:  By Subway - Take the L train to the Grand Street Station (at Bushwick Avenue), then walk 3 blocks along Grand Street to the studio.

By Car - From Manhattan, take the Williamsburg Bridge, be in the right lane on the Brooklyn side and heading straight off the bridge onto S. 5th Street.  Turn left on Havemeyer Street, go 2 blocks and then turn right onto Borinquen Place.  Borinquen Place turns into Grand Street, and go to the corner of Manhattan Avenue.

The Bronx Social  -  Held once per month on the 4th Saturday at the Nieves Latin Dance Studio, 1215 Morris Avenue (near East 168th Street), in the Morrisania-Concourse-South Bronx neighborhood of the Bronx, NY  10456.  Studio phone 718-590-9000.

How to get there:  By subway - Take the D or B train to the 167th Street Station.  Then walk east on E. 167th Street for 4 blocks to Morris Avenue.  By Car  -  Use Google Maps for the route by car.

For more information on public transportation, click on NYC Subway-Bus-Train Maps , or NY-NJ PATH Train Maps or Long Island Railroad Maps or MetroNorth Railroad Maps or HopStop.
 
OTHER INFORMATION

Nieves Latin Dance Studio teaches salsa to adults and children in classes and private lessons.  They also train for students to be in their performance teams.  For more information on classes, schedule and fees, go to www.NievesDanceStudio.com .
