TYPE OF EVENT & VENUE A classic On 2 salsa social held in a large dance studio with wooden floor & pro sound system.
MUSIC Almost entirely pure classic salsa plus a little cha cha, bachata & merengue.
DANCE PERFORMANCES Usually.  They would be listed on our Main Calendar.

PRICES Prices subject to change.  Usually $10 in advance, $12 at the door.  Possibly more or less depending on the occasion.  See flyer above for prices on a specific date.  Occasionally there is free admission.
BEVERAGES AND FOOD A selection of beverages is available for cash or credit card.  Sometimes there are drink specials and complementary food.
DRESS CODE Casual

DANCE TIMING

Almost entirely "New York On 2" timing and style.
DESCRIPTION The Balmir Latin Dance Studio teaches mambo On 2 "NY style" as well as other dances which are currently popular.  It has been expanding in terms of classes, students, instructors and performance teams.  They now have several performing groups which have danced in many locations in the NY metropolitan area.

They invite the salsa dancers from all over to join them at their monthly socials which usually feature performances by guest teams as well as their own, as well as many hours of classic salsa for the social dancers.
 
HOW TO GET THERE Held at the Balmir Latin Dance Studio, 959 Flatbush Avenue (between Snyder Avenue & Albemarle Road), 2nd floor, in the Flatbush neighborhood of Brooklyn, 11226.  Click Here For Map .

The closest train is the Q train to the Church Avenue Station.  Then walk along Church Avenue, past Ocean Avenue, to Flatbush Avenue.  Turn right and walk 1 1/2 blocks to the studio, which is on your left, up on the 2nd floor.  There are also MTA city buses which run along Flatbush and Church Avenues.

For more information on public transportation, click on NYC Subway-Bus-Train Maps , or NY-NJ PATH Train Maps or Long Island Railroad Maps or MetroNorth Railroad Maps or HopStop.
 
OTHER INFORMATION Balmir Latin Dance Studio offers classes in salsa, cha cha, merengue, bachata, kizomba, hustle and more.  To find out about their classes and events, contact them at phone - 718-462-1505 - email - balmirlatindancestudio@gmail.com - web site - www.BalmirLatinDanceStudio.com .
