MDN LATIN DANCE STUDIO SALSA SOCIAL
@ LA QUINTA INN & SUITES HARMON MEADOW

DATE OF EVENT Usually held on certain Saturdays, but occasionally on another day.  See our Main Calendar for exact dates.
TIME Doors open 9:00 PM for turn pattern class with MDN instructor.  Social dancing runs from 10:00 PM to 1:30 AM.

LOCATION

 Held in the ballroom of La Quinta Inn & Suites Harmon Meadow, 350 Lighting Way, Secaucus, NJ 07094.  Plenty of free parking across the street in Parking Lot D.
VIEW LOCATION MAP Click Here For Map .  Travel instructions are below.
PRESENTED BY Local On 2 instructor and performer Marcus Nieves of the MDN Latin Dance Studio .
CONTACT INFO Marcus Nieves.  Phone 201-388-7832.  Email MarcusDNieves@aol.com .  Web site www.MDNLatinDanceStudio.com  .
TYPE OF EVENT & VENUE This is a salsa social presented by an "On 2" dance studio, but all salsa dancers are invited.  It is held in a large ballroom with a lot of dancing space on a wooden dance floor which is "medium speed" and plenty of extra space for relaxing and socializing with tables and chairs.  There is a cash bar.  Plenty of free parking across the street in Parking Lot D.
MUSIC Almost entirely pure classic salsa plus a little cha cha, bachata & merengue.
DANCE PERFORMANCES Yes.  They are listed on our Main Calendar.
DJ Usually salsa DJ Ray Colon of U-Neek-Sounds Entertainment or guest DJ.  See our Main Calendar for DJ for a particular night.
BAND None.
DANCE FLOOR Large wooden dance floor which is "medium speed".
PRICES Regular admission is $15 - $20.
BEVERAGES AND FOOD Full cash bar with beer, wine and cocktails plus non-alcoholic beverages at reasonable prices. BYOB, no coolers.
DRESS CODE Casual but neat.

DANCE TIMING

Mostly "New York On 2" timing and style plus people of all timings are invited.
DESCRIPTION This pure salsa social is presented by Marcus Nieves of MDN Latin Dance Studio in New Jersey.  Marcus has been a performer and instructor in our NY/NJ mambo scene for years.  In the past he performed with “Fuerza Latina Dancers” in many events around the tri-state area, which provided him the exposure he needed to be recognized in the dance scene.  In 2000, Marcus began dancing under Nelson Flores and his dance team “Descarga Latina Dance Company” which opened the opportunity for him to take part in the off-Broadway show “Latin Madness”.  In late 2002, Marcus worked as one of the “Copa Boys” for the world famous “Copacabana” and in 2003 he become a member of the “Eddie Torres Dance Company” for which he continues to dance and tour around the world. 

This social is a meeting place for dancers of all levels to party to the sounds of classic DJ Ray Colon and to hang out with other partners and dancers who share the same passion for dancing mambo.
 
HOW TO GET THERE Held in the ballroom of La Quinta Inn & Suites Harmon Meadow, 350 Lighting Way, Secaucus, NJ 07094.  Plenty of free parking across the street in Parking Lot D.  Click Here For Map .  To get there by car from Manhattan:  Take the Lincoln Tunnel to New Jersey.  Then get on Route 3 and follow Google Map directions on your smartphone.

For more information on public transportation, click on NYC Subway-Bus-Train Maps , or NY-NJ PATH Train Maps or Long Island Railroad Maps or MetroNorth Railroad Maps .
 
OTHER INFORMATION The MDN Latin Dance Studio teaches beginner, intermediate and advanced classes in On 2 mambo.  It is located at 289 Main Street in the State Fair Mall, Belleville, NJ  07109.  Phone 201-451-0876 and 201-388-7832.  Email MarcusDNieves@aol.com .  Web site www.MarcusNieves.com .
