SALSA FEVER ON 2 SOCIAL

 DATE OF EVENT Usually held on the 4th Saturday of each month, though occasionally on other days.
TIME Usually 9:00 PM to 2:00 AM.  Sometimes the times will be different so see our Main Calendar for exact times.

LOCATION

 Held at the Salsa Fever On 2 Dance Studio, 83 Franklin Street, Jersey City, NJ  07307.  Phone 201-792-1616.
VIEW LOCATION MAP Click Here For Map .  Travel instructions are below.
PRESENTED BY Instructor and performer "Mambo Mario B", director of the Salsa Fever On 2 Dance Academy.
CONTACT INFO Contact Mario at email - MarioB@SalsaFeverOn2.com , phones:  Studio 201-792-1616, Cell 201- 851-7585.  Web site - www.SalsaFeverOn2.com .  See his SalsaNewYork.com instructor listings by clicking on Mario. 
TYPE OF EVENT & VENUE An On 2 mambo social held in a very attractive dance studio with a large wooden dance floor.  This is the regular mambo social for the Salsa Fever On 2 Dance Academy and also for the wider salsa dancer community, with dancers invited from New Jersey, New York and Connecticut. 
MUSIC Almost entirely pure classic salsa plus a little cha cha, bachata & merengue. 
DANCE PERFORMANCES Very occasionally. They would be listed on our Main Calendar.
DJ DJ Mario B and DJ JaviChuela.
BAND No.
DANCE FLOOR Large wooden dance floor which is "medium speed".
PRICES $10 admission. 
BEVERAGES AND FOOD Refreshments available.
DRESS CODE Casual.

DANCE TIMING

Almost entirely "New York On 2" timing and style.
DESCRIPTION A pure mambo dancer's social brought to us by NJ performer, instructor and all-around nice guy Mario Gonzalez, AKA "Mambo Mario B".  There may be dance performances at some socials to be announced.  See our Main Calendar for performers names & dates.  This event is now being held at Mario B's own studio with a large wooden dance floor, excellent professional sound system and reasonable prices.   There will be music by DJ Mario B and DJ JaviChuela.  Lots of On 2 dancer partners are invited from the tri-state area mambo community.  This is an easy drive from NYC through the Lincoln or Holland Tunnels. 
 
HOW TO GET THERE

Held at the Salsa Fever On 2 Dance Studio, 83 Franklin Street, Jersey City, NJ  07307.  Phone 201-792-1616.  Click Here For Map .

To get there from Manhattan by public transportation - Take the PATH train to Journal Square Station or Hoboken Bus Terminal, then take th #87 bus to Franklin Street, and stop at the corner of Franklin St. & Hancock St.  Walk 1 block down towards Webster ST. (laundromat) and the studio is located on the right side across the street from the laundromat. 

By car - Click Here For Map , then select "directions" and put in your point of origin.

For more information on public transportation, click on  NYC Subway-Bus-Train Maps , or NY-NJ PATH Train Maps or Long Island Railroad Maps or MetroNorth Railroad Maps .
 
OTHER INFORMATION

And  "Mambo Mario B" always says is:  "Don't move to the music.  Let the music move you."
