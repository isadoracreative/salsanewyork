SALSA UNION NYC - NDD EDITION

@ STEPPING OUT STUDIOS

    

DATE OF EVENT Usually held on the 3rd Saturday of each month @ Stepping Out Studios
TIME Social dancing usually 9:00 PM to 3:00 AM, but sometimes the hours may be changed, so see our Main Calendar for the correct time for a particular date.  Usually bachata class 8:00 PM, salsa class 9:00 PM, held in separate room.

LOCATION

 Held at Stepping Out Studios, 37 West 26th Street (between 6th Avenue & Broadway), 9th floor, Manhattan, NYC. 
VIEW LOCATION MAP Click Here For Map.  Travel instructions are below.
 
PRESENTED BY NYC On 2 mambo DJ El Maestro .
CONTACT INFO  DJ El Maestro. - Email - ElMaestro4U@hotmail.com, Phone - 347-522-6320.  Web Sites - SalsaUnion FaceBook Page,  www.DJAlexElMaestro.com and www.CandelaFridays.com and www.SalsaManiaSaturdays.com .
 
TYPE OF EVENT & VENUE This is a popular classic mambo social held in a very large dance studio, with "medium speed" wooden floor.  It is well attended by On 2 dancers and there is plenty of seating plus refreshments available for purchase.
 
MUSIC Salsa, guaguanco, guarachas, charanga, mambo, cha cha, boogaloo, pachangas, plus a little bachata.  DJ El Maestro will occasionally spin your favorite tunes from his personal vinyl collection.
DANCE PERFORMANCES Usually.  They will be listed on our Main Calendar.
DJ DJ El Maestro plus occasional invited guest DJs. 
BAND No.
DANCE FLOOR Very large professional wooden ballroom dance floor which is "medium speed".
PRICES $15 general admission.  More for special events.  See above flyer for prices.
BEVERAGES AND FOOD

Cash beverages are available, such as water, etc.
DRESS CODE Casual.

DANCE TIMING

Almost entirely "New York On 2" timing and style.
DESCRIPTION

Since his start, DJ El Maestro has quickly risen in popularity among New York salseros, DJing in local venues such as Mambo Fateegz, the Jimmy Anton Social, Club Cache, Club Iguana, the Santo Rico Social, Rumbamba Saturdays, the Brooklyn Salsa Congress in 2009 and 2010, the 10th Annual New York Salsa Congress, the Big Apple Salsa Festival and outside of NY in major venues like the Houston Salsa Congress, Connecticut Salsa Fest, the Peru Salsa Congress. 

This Salsa Union event is held in a very large professional ballroom dance studio with plenty of room for dancing and socializing.  All the mambo dancers are invited.  There is photo and video coverage by Chomio.
 
HOW TO GET THERE The event is being held at the Stepping Out Studios, 37 West 26th Street (between Broadway and 6th Avenue/aka Avenue of the Americas).   For exact location on a map which you can zoom in and out, Click Here For Map.  Closest subway stations are the 23rd or 28th Street Stations on the N, R or W lines, and also the 23rd Street Station on the F or V lines.  It is usually fairly easy to park within a few blocks if you drive around a few minutes.  But read the parking signs very carefully.  Some streets have Night Parking Restrictions.

For more information on public transportation, click on NYC Subway-Bus-Train Maps , or NY-NJ PATH Train Maps or Long Island Railroad Maps or MetroNorth Railroad Maps .

 
OTHER INFORMATION

DJ El Maestro has two other events also:  Candela Fridays NYC @ DanceSport Studios and SalsaMania Saturdays @ DanceSport Studios .
