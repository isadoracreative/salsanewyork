LA VIEJA GUARDIA SALSA SOCIAL

              
                                                                                                                        
  See Promo Video for this event

DATE OF EVENT Usually held twice per month on the 2nd and 4th Sunday.  See our Main Calendar for exact dates.
TIME 6:00 - 10:00 PM.

LOCATION

Held at Stepping Out Studios, 37 West 26th Street (between 6th Avenue & Broadway), 9th floor, Manhattan, NYC.  Studio phone - 646-742-9400.
 
VIEW LOCATION MAP

Click Here For Map .  Travel instructions are below..
 
PRESENTED BY DJ Antonio "La Conga" and instructor / performer Joel Dominguez.
CONTACT INFO Web site - www.LVGNewYork.com .

Joel Dominguez - 718-924-9425.  Email - JoelSalsa@gmail.com.  Web site www.JoelSalsa.com .

Antonio "La Conga" - 347-248-2359.  Email - AntonioLaConga@gmail.com , web site www.DJLaConga.com .
TYPE OF EVENT & VENUE Classic salsa social held in a very large dance studio ballroom with plenty of On 2 partners ranging from beginner to advanced.
MUSIC Entirely pure classic salsa, mambo and a little cha cha played on an excellent sound system, sometimes from the original vinyl records..
DANCE PERFORMANCES Sometimes.  They will be listed on our Main Calendar.
DJ DJ Antonio "La Conga" and occasionally an invited guest DJ.  See our Main Calendar for guest DJ.
BAND None.
DANCE FLOOR Very large wooden studio dance floor which is generally "medium speed".  However, when the weather and the dancing get really hot, the floor can become a bit slower, so you may want to wear your more slippery shoes.
PRICES $15 general admission all night from 6:00 - 10:00 PM.  Before 7:00 PM, the price is $13 but only with VIP Text or Email by going to http://lvgnewyork.com/ .
BEVERAGES AND FOOD Beverages for sale such as water, sports drinks, sodas.  Feel free to BYOB.
DRESS CODE Very casual.  Come to dance hard.

DANCE TIMING

Entirely "New York On 2" timing and style.
DESCRIPTION

The salseros putting on this event, DJ Antonio "La Conga" and instructor / performer Joel Dominguez, are both members of our local mambo community. DJ Antonio "La Conga" is a local DJ specializing in the classic salsa music, even collecting songs that are only available on original vinyl records.  And Joel Dominguez is a popular local dancer who teaches and performs as well.

They invite all the dancers to come and enjoy this classic salsa social where the emphasis is on the pure music and dancing that we all love.  The event is held in a very large dance studio ballroom with an excellent wooden dance floor and a professional sound system.  This event has become one of the most consistently popular socials in the NYC On 2 community.
 
HOW TO GET THERE The event is being held at the Stepping Out Studios, 37 West 26th Street (between Broadway and 6th Avenue/aka Avenue of the Americas).   For exact location on a map which you can zoom in and out, Click Here For Map.  Closest subway stations are the 23rd or 28th Street Stations on the N, R or W lines, and also the 23rd Street Station on the F or V lines.  It is usually fairly easy to park within a few blocks if you drive around a few minutes.

For more information on public transportation, click on NYC Subway-Bus-Train Maps , or NY-NJ PATH Train Maps or Long Island Railroad Maps or MetroNorth Railroad Maps .
 
OTHER INFORMATION

Joel Dominguez, aka Joel "Salsa" D, teaches mambo at all levels.  To contact him for lessons, his phone is 718-924-9425.  Email - JoelSalsa@gmail.com .  Web page www.JoelSalsa.com  .
