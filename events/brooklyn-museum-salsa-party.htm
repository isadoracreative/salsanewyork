BROOKLYN MUSEUM
SALSA PARTY

DATE OF EVENT Usually held on the 2nd Thursday of the month, although sometimes on other dates.  See our Main Calendar for exact dates.

Upcoming 2018 dates on Thursdays:  January 11th, February 8th, March 8th, April 19th.
TIME

6:00 to 9:30 PM.  Dance lesson 6:00, social dancing 6:45 to 9:30 PM.

LOCATION

 Held at the Brooklyn Museum, 200 Eastern Parkway (near Washington Avenue), Brooklyn, NY, 11238.  It will take place either in the ground floor glass pavilion area of the museum, or upstairs on the 3rd floor at the Beaux-Arts Court.
VIEW LOCATION MAP Click Here For Map.  Travel instructions are below.
PRESENTED BY Balmir Latin Dance Studio .
CONTACT INFO Balmir Latin Dance Studio - phone - 718-462-1505 - email - balmirlatindancestudio@gmail.com - web site - www.BalmirLatinDanceStudio.com .  See FaceBook Page For This Event .
TYPE OF EVENT & VENUE Large area for dancing.  There is a basic salsa lesson early in the evening followed by performances and social dancing to DJ music on a pro sound system.   It will take place either in the ground floor glass pavilion area of the museum, or upstairs on the 3rd floor at the Beaux-Arts Court.
MUSIC Almost entirely pure classic salsa plus a little cha cha, bachata & merengue.
DANCE PERFORMANCES TBA.  See our Main Calendar for names of performers.
DJ TBA.  See our Main Calendar for names of DJs.
BAND No.
DANCE FLOOR Good size dance floors.
PRICES Free admission.  A donation to the museum is requested.
BEVERAGES AND FOOD There is a cash beverage and snack bar in the museum.
DRESS CODE Casual.

DANCE TIMING

Almost entirely "New York On 2" timing and style.
DESCRIPTION Several times a year, this event is held at the Brooklyn Museum with a good size dance floor area.  There is a basic salsa lesson early in the evening followed by performances and social dancing to DJ music on a pro sound system.  This venue is an interesting change from our usual On 2 locations, usually held in a dance studio or club.  And if you want to arrive a bit early, you can spend some time looking at the exhibits in the huge Brooklyn Museum.

The event is very well attended by On 2 mambo regulars and new-comers.  Including a lesson, performances and DJ for social dancing, it is a nice way for a dancer to spend a Thursday evening.
 
HOW TO GET THERE Held at the Brooklyn Museum, 200 Eastern Parkway (near Washington Avenue), Brooklyn, NY, 11238.  It is held on the 3rd floor in the Beaux-Arts Court.  Click Here For Map . 

Very convenient to get to by public transportation. Subway - Take the # 2 or #3 train to the Eastern Parkway / Brooklyn Museum Station.

By car:  Take the Manhattan or Brooklyn Bridge and get onto Flatbush Avenue heading outbound from Manhattan.  At the traffic circle at Grand Army Plaza, take Eastern Parkway which goes along the side of Prospect Park.  The Brooklyn Museum will be on your right.

For more information on public transportation, click on NYC Subway-Bus-Train Maps , or NY-NJ PATH Train Maps or Long Island Railroad Maps or MetroNorth Railroad Maps or HopStop.
