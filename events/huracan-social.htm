HURACAN SOCIAL

@ THE HURACAN DANCE STUDIO

Please Note:  The January 27, 2018, 6th Anniversary Social
will be held at the Queens Theater, 14 United Nations Avenue S.,
Queens, NY 11368.  Click Here for Facebook listing for this event.

TYPE OF EVENT & VENUE An On 2 mambo social held in a fairly large studio with a wooden dance floor and a separate room for bachata.
MUSIC Almost entirely pure classic salsa plus a little cha cha, bachata & merengue.  There is a separate bachata room.
DANCE PERFORMANCES Sometimes.  They would be listed on our Main Calendar.
DJ Usually DJ Chuleria, Rich Rodriguez, and Abel Taveras plus invited guest DJs.  See our Main Calendar for name of DJ(s) for a particular night.
BAND None.
DANCE FLOOR One fairly large wooden dance floor which is "medium to fast speed", so don't wear your slippery shoes.
PRICES Usually $12.  Prices subject to change.
BEVERAGES AND FOOD Various beverages are available for sale.
DRESS CODE Casual

DANCE TIMING

Almost entirely "New York On 2" timing and style.
DESCRIPTION The Huracan Dance Studio is derived from one of the pioneers of the New York style On 2 mambo, the  Santo Rico Dance Company.  For more than a decade, the studio in Manhattan stood for great dancing, excellent technique, classic style and complex rhythmic choreography.  Their Santo Rico Dancers team performed and did workshops all over the world.

This event in their Huracan Dance Studio is their regular social, usually featuring 1 or 2 dance teams and, of course, lots of classic salsa music for your many hours of dancing.  There will be plenty of dance partners at all levels, from beginner to advanced.  Santo Rico invites everyone to come and party with them.
