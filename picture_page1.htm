JUAN PORTELLA

Mambo instructor in the Bronx and at Dance On 2 studio in Manhattan, Copa Dancer, and long-time salsero in our mambo community.

Picture and description below from www.DanceOn2.com .

"Juan's unique style of dancing has brought him to perform on stage for hundreds of people in off Broadway plays and in prestigious places such as Madison Square Garden Theater. Many of the stages were shared with singing sensations like Kevin Ceballo, El Canario, El Gran Combo, and Tito Rojas, just to name a few. He has also been featured on various TV networks like channel 41 and Bronxnet, Juan travels nationally to conduct workshops and is currently one of the lead dancers at the world famous Copacabana night club."

ZULMA RODRIGUEZ

Dance Instructor
Email - ZRodriguez@nyc.rr.com
Phone - 646-678-0687

Zulma has been performing professionally since the age of 7.  Her parents through their Puerto Rican culture passed down the love of dance. She studied Jazz at The Dance Academy in Rockville Centre as a teenager.  She and another dancer started a dance company named Dance Quest.  The dance company performed at bar mitzvahs and weddings with many major bands all over the country.  She and another dancer performed with a major band at an important function in St. Marten the show was televised and in the local newspapers.  Zulma continues to perform solo and also with a dance partner for special events. Zulma began teaching hip-hop, and hustle classes 13 years ago at Starr Dance Studio in Melville Long Island.  She was trained in ballroom dancing and was a teacher at Dance Obsession in Farmingdale Long Island.  Zulma is also a certified fitness instructor, and a psychotherapist.  She currently teaches at You Should Be Dancing Studio in Manhattan - www.YouShouldBeDancing.com .  This write-up is taken from their instructor information.
