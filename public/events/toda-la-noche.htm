TODA LA NOCHE @ STEPPING OUT STUDIOS

DATE OF EVENT Held on the 1st Saturday of each month and occasionally on another night.  It would be listed on our Main Calendar.
TIME 9:00 PM to 3:00 AM or later. 

LOCATION

 Held at Stepping Out Studios, 37 West 26th Street (between 6th Avenue & Broadway), 9th floor, Manhattan, NYC. 
VIEW LOCATION MAP Click Here For Map.  Travel instructions are below.
PRESENTED BY Presented by Nick Messina of Bongo Loco .  See FaceBook Page.
CONTACT INFO Nick Messina - Email - BongoLoco1@gmail.com , Phone - 516-852-5046.
TYPE OF EVENT & VENUE This is a popular classic mambo social held in a very large dance studio, fixed up with tables & lighting like a nightclub, with "medium speed" wooden floor.  It is well attended by On 2 dancers and there is plenty of seating plus refreshments available for purchase.
MUSIC Almost entirely pure classic salsa plus a little cha cha, bachata & merengue.
DANCE PERFORMANCES Occasionally.  They would be listed on our Main Calendar.
DJ DJ Rudy "The Power Of Love" Acosta and DJ Bongo Loco aka Nick Messina , plus occasional invited guest DJs.
BAND Occasionally.  They would be listed on our Main Calendar.
DANCE FLOOR Held in a very large dance studio with "medium speed" wooden floor.
PRICES $15 admission.  Prices subject to change for special occasions.
BEVERAGES AND FOOD Beverages are for sale - water, juice, soda, sports drinks, etc. 
DRESS CODE Casual, standard dress for a mambo social in a dance studio.

DANCE TIMING

Mostly "New York On 2" timing and style.
DESCRIPTION

Fellow dancer and On 2 instructor Nick Messina of Bongo Loco has been running this popular classic mambo social for more than 5 years.  All dancers are invited to come party in the very large Stepping Out ballroom.  The atmosphere is casual and friendly, kind of like a salsa nightclub party.  The room is decorated like a nightclub, with low colored lighting and cloth covered tables.  Water, sodas and sports drinks, etc. are sold. 

This event is held in the very large Stepping Out Studios where many of our On 2 events have been held in the past.  The dance floor is huge with plenty of room for all the dancers.  It is a professional-level wooden floor that is "medium speed".  The sound system is big and professional and the atmosphere is very friendly with regulars and newcomers too.  The full range of dancers attend, from beginner to advanced.
 
HOW TO GET THERE The event is being held at the Stepping Out Studios, 37 West 26th Street (between Broadway and 6th Avenue/aka Avenue of the Americas).   For exact location on a map which you can zoom in and out, Click Here For Map.  Closest subway stations are the 23rd or 28th Street Stations on the N, R or W lines, and also the 23rd Street Station on the F or V lines.  It is usually fairly easy to park within a few blocks if you drive around a few minutes.

For more information on public transportation, click on NYC Subway-Bus-Train Maps , or NY-NJ PATH Train Maps or Long Island Railroad Maps or MetroNorth Railroad Maps .
 
OTHER INFORMATION

Nick Messina, who runs this event, is an On 2 mambo instructor.  For information on his classes and privates, contact him at Email - BongoLoco1@gmail.com , Phone - 516-852-5046.
