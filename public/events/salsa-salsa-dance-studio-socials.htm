SALSA SALSA DANCE STUDIO SOCIALS

DATE OF EVENT

Held on the 1st, 3rd & 5th Friday of each month.  See our Main Calendar for exact dates.
TIME 9:00 PM to 2:00 AM or later.

LOCATION

Held near their Park Slope studio at The Cherry Tree (restaurant and bar), 65 4th Avenue (between Bergen St. & St. Marks Place), in the Park Slope neighborhood of Brooklyn, NY  11217.  718-399-1353.
VIEW LOCATION MAP

Click Here For Map.  Travel instructions are below.
PRESENTED BY Marcus Gonzalez and Jose Rivera, partners & directors of the Salsa Salsa Dance Studios.
CONTACT INFO Jose Rivera  -  Email - info@salsasalsadancestudio.com  -  Phone 718-602-1322 and 718-408-0477.

Marcus Gonzalez - Phones:  718-602-1322 and 917-974-1568.  Email:  SouLatin@aol.com . 
TYPE OF EVENT & VENUE An On 2 mambo social held in a neighborhood restaurant / bar with 1 medium size room, dance floor, tables & chairs.  The crowd ranges from 40 to 100 dancers.  The size of the crowd depends on whether it's a regular social or a special event with several performances or other special attractions, and depending on whether there are many dancers from the wider mambo community attending the event.
MUSIC Mostly pure classic salsa plus a little cha cha, bachata & merengue.
DANCE PERFORMANCES Occasionally. They would be listed on our Main Calendar.
DJ Rotating DJs to be announced.
BAND None
DANCE FLOOR Medium size wooden floor.
PRICES Free.
BEVERAGES AND FOOD Beverages such as water, Gatorade, sodas & Red Bull are for sale as well as a full bar.  They specialize in a wide selection of beers on tap and in bottles.  See Beer Menu.  There is typical pub food available all night.
DRESS CODE Casual

DANCE TIMING

Almost entirely "New York On 2" timing and style.
DESCRIPTION Founded in 2004, the Salsa Salsa Dance Studio is now a well-established salsa school which is an active part of the Brooklyn mambo community.  Under partners Marcus Gonzalez and Jose Rivera, plus instructor Ray Colon and others, the studio has now moved all its activities to it's Park Slope location.  Their students come from Brooklyn and elsewhere to participate in their classes and socials.  All dancers, students and more advanced dancers, are invited to the socials which feature a friendly welcoming environment and lots of good salsa dance music.  This is one of the "Brooklyn circle of salsa studios" which have grown very popular in the last several years spreading mambo On 2 throughout the borough.

Held in a local restaurant with full bar and casual food served all evening.
 
HOW TO GET THERE

Held near their Park Slope studio at The Cherry Tree (restaurant and bar), 65 4th Avenue (between Bergen St. & St. Marks Place), in the Park Slope neighborhood of Brooklyn, NY  11217.  718-399-1353.  Click Here For Map .

To get there by subway:  N, R, D, Q, B, 2, 3, 4, 5 & Long Island RR trains to the Atlantic Avenue / Barclays Center Stations.  Then walk a couple of blocks on 4th Avenue to Cherry Tree.  To get there by car:  Manhattan or Brooklyn Bridges to Brooklyn.  Then take Flatbush Avenue.  Turn right onto 4th Avenue and go a couple of blocks to the studio.

See the Travel Directions on their web site.  For more information on public transportation, click on NYC Subway-Bus-Train Maps , or NY-NJ PATH Train Maps or Long Island Railroad Maps or MetroNorth Railroad Maps .
 
OTHER INFORMATION The Salsa Salsa Dance Studio teaches salsa/mambo to adults and children.  To get information about the classes, the schedule and prices, see their web site at www.SalsaSalsaDanceStudio.com .  The web site has complete biographies of the teaching staff - click on Instructor Listings.
