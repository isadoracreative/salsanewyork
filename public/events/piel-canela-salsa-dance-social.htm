PIEL CANELA SALSA DANCE SOCIAL

 

DATE OF EVENT Usually every 2nd & 4th Saturday of each month.
TIME 7:00 PM to 11:00 PM.  

LOCATION

 This dance party is being held at Pearl Studios, 500 8th Avenue (between 35th & 36th Street), 12th Floor, in midtown Manhattan, NYC.  Studio phone 212-924-6160
VIEW LOCATION MAP Click Here For Map .  Travel instructions are below.
PRESENTED BY Joe Burgos, instructor, performer, Director of the Piel Canela Dance & Music School, and Piel Canela Dance Company.
CONTACT INFO Phone 212-924-6160 email  joe@pielcaneladancers.com.  Another contact is  bianca@pielcaneladancers.com.  Also, see the www.PielCanelaDancers.com web site and the  Dance Social Page.
TYPE OF EVENT & VENUE Held in a large dance studio with excellent wooden dance floor, mirror walls, sound system.
MUSIC Almost entirely pure classic salsa plus some Cha-Cha and Bachata as well.
DANCE PERFORMANCES Occasionally. They would be listed on our Main Calendar.
DJ DJ Joe Burgos/DJ Cocotaso and occasional invited guest DJ's.
BAND Occasionally. They would be listed on our Main Calendar.
DANCE FLOOR Very large excellent wooden dance floor which is "medium speed".
PRICES
If there is no band general admission is $12 online and $15 at the door.

If there is a band , general admission is $15-20 online $20-$25 at the door
BEVERAGES AND FOOD Water and other beverages are sold. 
DRESS CODE Very casual, come as you are.  On special occasions, dress code will change and be listed for that day/

DANCE TIMING

Entirely "New York On 2" timing and style.
DESCRIPTION

Another pure dancers event hosted by On 2  instructor/performer/choreographer Joe Burgos, Director of Piel Canela Dancers & Dance Studio and also the Piel Canela Dance Company.  These socials will  usually be held on the 2nd & 4th Saturday of each month.  There may be either a dance group performing  or a band which will be listed on our Calendar for dates & details.  There will be a maximum amount of salsa dance music played by a DJ (see Calendar for name of DJ) and the event is being held in a large size studio which has an excellent medium-speed wooden dance floor and is fully air conditioned.

Click on Piel Canela Social Pictures to see photographs of this event by Amir Shimoni on his web site www.MamboPics.com .
