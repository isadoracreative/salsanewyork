LATIN FRIDAYS

@ SALSA IN QUEENS

DATE OF EVENT Held on the last Friday of each month.
TIME 9:00 PM to 2:00 AM

LOCATION

 Held at the Salsa In Queens Dance Studio, 34-27 Steinway Street (between 34th & 34th Avenues), street level store-front, Astoria, NY (Queens), 11103.  Studio phone 347-669-3589.
VIEW LOCATION MAP Click Here For Map.  Travel instructions are below.
PRESENTED BY Jean Franco Vergaray, founder & director of Salsa In Queens.
CONTACT INFO Phone 347-669-3589, Email SalsaInQueens@gmail.com , Web site www.SalsaInQueens.com .  Facebook page https://www.facebook.com/SalsaInQueens/ .
TYPE OF EVENT & VENUE  A classic salsa social held in a newly built dance studio with excellent wood floor & pro sound system.
MUSIC Mostly pure classic salsa plus a little cha cha, bachata & merengue.
DANCE PERFORMANCES Very occasionally. They would be listed on our Main Calendar.
DJ "DJ Negro Malo" - Nelson Cruz.
BAND None.
DANCE FLOOR One good size "medium speed" wood dance floor.
PRICES $15 in advance (before 5:30 on the day of the social), $20 at the door.  Part of the admission money goes to charity.
BEVERAGES AND FOOD Cash beverages available.
DRESS CODE Casual

DANCE TIMING

Almost entirely "New York On 2" timing and style.
DESCRIPTION These events are classic On 2 mambo socials held at the newly built Salsa In Queens dance studio on Steinway Street, one of the main streets in Astoria Queens, with the subway station just 1/2 a block nearby.  Jean Franco Vergaray hosts this party and invites all the mambo dancers to come and party; all are welcome.  He is the founder and director of Salsa In Queens.  They also hold salsa events in nearby restaurants and clubs on various evenings of the week.  See the Events Section of their web site www.SalsaInQueens.com for a complete listing.  The music at this social is by professional "DJ Negro Malo" - Nelson Cruz played on a pro sound system.  The social starts out with a class taught by Jean Franco Vergaray followed by dancing until 2:00 AM.  The motto for their parties is "We sure know how to have fun."
 
HOW TO GET THERE Held at the Salsa In Queens Dance Studio, 34-27 Steinway Street (between 34th & 34th Avenues), street level store-front, Astoria, NY (Queens), 11103.  Studio phone 347-669-3589.  Click Here For Map .

Closest subway is the M & R trains to the Steinway Street station 1/2 block away from the studio.
The studio is only a couple of blocks from the intersection of Northern Blvd. & Steinway Street if you are coming by car.  Easiest route from Manhattan would be across the Queensboro Bridge (lower roadway), then onto Northern Blvd, then turn left onto Steinway Street and go a couple of blocks.  The studio would be on your right.

For more information on public transportation, click on NYC Subway-Bus-Train Maps , or NY-NJ PATH Train Maps or Long Island Railroad Maps or MetroNorth Railroad Maps or HopStop.
 
OTHER INFORMATION

Salsa In Queens offers a full schedule of classes for salsa and bachata throughout the week.  See their Class Schedule and see their contact information above.
