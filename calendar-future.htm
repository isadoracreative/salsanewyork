FUTURE LOCAL SALSA EVENTS

For more information on each event, click your mouse on the name of the club or event which is WRITTEN IN BLUE & UNDERLINED

REGULAR FUTURE EVENTS:

LATIN SUNDAYS PARTY WITH JIMMY ANTON - Downtown Manhattan, NYC - 5:00 - 9:00 PM - 1st, 3rd & 5th Sundays - The longest running & most attended On 2 mambo social in New York City since 1993 - A destination for dancers from all over the world - Excellent studio floor & hundreds of dance partners for you to choose from - Presented by long-time mambo instructor & performer Jimmy Anton, who is the main DJ, playing classic salsa & cha cha.

MAMBO SUNDAYS PARTY WITH CARLOS KONIG - Midtown Manhattan, NYC - 5:30 - 9:30 PM on the 2nd & 4th Sunday of each month -  An On 2 social that has been running for many years in a friendly welcoming atmosphere - Carlos invites us for nonstop dancing & socializing in a large modern studio with 3 rooms with a perfect wooden floor that never gets sticky - Lots of  classic salsa & cha cha music on an excellent pro DJ sound system by DJ Carlos Konig plus guest DJs - Held at Ripley Grier Studios in midtown Manhattan - Free snacks & cash beverages are available

PIEL CANELA SALSA DANCE SOCIAL - NYC - 7:00 - 11:00 PM - Usually held on the 2nd & 4th Saturdays of each month - An On 2 event with lots of classic salsa in the spacious ballroom of Pearl Studios by DJ Cocotaso / Joe Burgos & guest DJs -  Presented by instructor & performer Joe Burgos, Director of the Piel Canela Dance Studio & the Piel Canela crew who invited all the dancers to come and party with them -

YAMULEE'S BOOGIE DOWN MAMBO PARTY - Fordham/Grand Concourse, Bronx - 10:00 PM - 2:00 AM or later - Usually held monthly.  See our Main Calendar for exact dates - Dance performance to be announced  - Another swinging affair presented by Osmar Perrones & the Yamulee crew - MC'd by famous Levi "La Leña" - Lots of great old & new salsa, plus a little good bachata & merengue, by DJ Osmar "El Matatan" Perrones ", DJ "Doc Salsa" Steve Shaw , DJ Amauri and DJ Jose - Everyone's invited to this classic uptown social with plenty of partners at all levels - One of the more popular events for the NYC On 2 community.  See our Main Calendar for exact dates.

SALSA FEVER ON 2 SOCIAL @ THE SALSA FEVER ON 2 DANCE ACADEMY - Jersey City, NJ - 9:00 PM - 2:00 AM - Usually on the 4th Saturday of the month - Instructor & performer Mario B presents this monthly pure mambo social in his very attractive dance studio with big wooden dance floor & pro sound system, a short ride from Manhattan - DJ Mario B & DJ JaviChuela play lots of classic salsa plus bit of bachata & more - All tri-state dancers are invited to come join the party

AN EVENING WITH ABAKUA - Midtown Manhattan, NYC - 10:00 PM - 2:00 AM - 4th Friday of each month - There is a boogaloo warm-up from 9:30 - 10:00 PM - A salsa dancing party held in the attractive "Club 412" of You Should Be Dancing Studios with beverages & full bar - Pure classic old school salsa & cha cha by DJ Frankie Martinez -  Hosted by Manny Blackett - An event presented by  Frankie Martinez of Abakua Afro-Latin Dancers - A pure On 2 mambo party attended by a wide range of dancers from all over, including some very good ones - Drink specials before 11:00 PM

SALSA SALSA DANCE STUDIO SOCIAL @ THE CHERRY TREE - Park Slope, Brooklyn - Held on the 1st, 3rd & 5th Fridays of the month - 9:00 PM - 2:00 AM or later - Free - Everyone is invited to this On 2 mambo social held at The Cherry Tree bar & restaurant near the Salsa Salsa Studio - Plenty of classic salsa music plus a little cha cha & more by DJ John John and DJ Andy Andy in a casual pub atmosphere with wooden dance floor - Presented by Marcus Gonzalez, Jose Rivera, Ray Colon & the Salsa Salsa crew

TUMBAO THURSDAYS @ YAYO'S - Downtown Brooklyn - 9:00 PM - 1:00 AM - A weekly Thursday salsa event held downstairs in the 2 room lounge of Yayo's, formerly El Viejo Yayo restaurant - Classic salsa & more by DJ John John plus live drummers - There is also a separate bachata room with DJ Jun - Full bar & late Latin dinner & appetizer menu - Presented by Marcus Gonzalez & Jose Rivera of the Salsa Salsa Dance Studio , & Que Viva La Salsa Entertainment, DJ John John, who invite everyone to this popular Thursday club night

LORENZ DANCE STUDIO  MAMBO SOCIAL -  Queens, Bronx & Manhattan - 9:00 PM - 12:00 midnight - A weekly On 2 mambo social presented by Howie Lorenz & the folks at Lorenz Dance Studio - Everyone is invited for classic salsa & cha cha in a friendly environment in a good size studio with wooden dance floor & friendly dance partners.  Held every Friday at their Glendale, Queens, and Manhattan locations, and held every Saturday at their Corona, Queens, and Bronx locations.

LVG - LA VIEJA GUARDIA  SALSA SOCIAL - Downtown Manhattan, NYC - 6:00 - 10:00 PM - Held on the 2nd & 4th Sunday of each month.  A pure mambo social featuring excellent classic salsa music including some from original vinyl recordings - Brought to us by local salseros  DJ Antonio "La Conga" and instructor / performer Joel Dominguez  - Lots of classic salsa & cha cha music by DJ Antonio "La Conga" - One of the most popular events for the NYC On 2 community with partners at all levels & held in a very large ballroom - See Promo Video for this event

BAILA WEDNESDAYS @ SOLAS - Downtown Manhattan - 8:30 PM - 1:00 AM or later - A popular pure mambo event held in a charming bar / club/ lounge setting featuring a night of classic salsa & cha cha plus a bit of bachata on a good sound system - Presented by DJ Babaloo of DJB-NYC.com for the dancers - Full bar with very reasonable drink prices & you can bring your own food in to eat - Salsa lessons starting at 7:00 PM adv. beginner  & 8:00 PM basic beginner by Dave Huang

MDN LATIN DANCE STUDIO SALSA SOCIAL @ LA QUINTA INN - Secaucus, NJ - 9:00 PM - 1:30 AM - Everyone is invited to this long-running social presented by instructor & performer Marcus Nieves of the MDN Latin Dance Studio - Held in a large hall with good floor, pro sound system, seating, full cash bar & free parking - Plenty of classic salsa by DJ Ray Colon of U-Neek-Sounds Entertainment - Salsa class by Matt Clemente 9:00 PM - Upcoming future dates - See our Main Calendar

SALSA MANIA SATURDAYS @ DANCESPORT STUDIOS - Midtown Manhattan - 8:00 PM - 4:00 AM- Usually held on the 2nd & 4th Saturdays of each month - This is a classic On 2 mambo social held in an attractive studio set up like a club with adjoining cafe & bar serving beverages & food - Excellent ballroom dance floor  - Presented by NYC salsa DJ El Maestro playing classic salsa music & more, inviting all the salseros  - See Promo Video - There is also a separate room with bachata music - Classes:  bachata 7:00 - 8:00 PM, salsa 8:00 - 9:00 PM .

CANDELA FRIDAYS NYC @ DANCESPORT STUDIOS - Midtown Manhattan - Usually 9:30 PM - 3:00 AM - Held on the 1st, 3rd & 5th Fridays of each month - Takes place in an attractive studio set up like a club with adjoining cafe serving beverages & food - This is a classic On 2 mambo social - Excellent ballroom dance floor  - Presented by NYC salsa DJ El Maestro playing classic salsa music plus a bit of bachata & merengue, who invites all the salseros to come & party - See Promo Video

SALSA UNION NYC - NDD EDITION @ STEPPING OUT STUDIOS - Downtown Manhattan, NYC - Held on the 3rd Saturday of each month - 9:00 PM - 3:00 AM - Dance performances to be announced - Classes held in separate room:  8:00 PM bachata and 9:00 PM salsa - Social dancing in the spacious main ballroom 9:00 PM - 3:00 AM with excellent dance floor - Presented by NYC salsa DJ El Maestro playing classic salsa music plus a bit more, who invites all the salseros to come & party at this monthly mambo social

BALMIR LATIN DANCE STUDIO SALSA SOCIAL - Flatbush, Brooklyn - 9:00 PM - 3:00 AM - Held on the 4th Saturday of every month - Dance performances TBA - A classic salsa social presented Eric Balmir & the crew at Balmir Latin Dance Studio - With plenty of classic salsa plus more played by DJ Alejandro - Held in a large dance studio with pro wooden floor & sound system - Everyone is invited - Future Dates: 

LAS CHICAS LOCAS @ DANCESPORT - Midtown Manhattan - Held every Sunday.  Usually 9:30 PM - 2:00 AM - A mambo event for those who want to keep on dancing after the regular Sunday socials - Held in an attractive dance studio with adjoining cafe & attended by a crowd of available partners - Presented by DJ Alejandro Bouza who plays a wide ranging mix of salsa plus a bit of cha cha & bachata -  From 5:00 - 9:00 PM there is a mostly bachata party.  Future 2015 dates with special features: 

SALSA ON 2'SDAYS - ROOFTOP PARTY @ THE COPACABANA - Midtown Manhattan, Times Square neighborhood, NYC - Held every Tuesday - 9:00 PM - 2:00 AM - Live bands every week plus DJ Rudy Acosta or guest DJ playing lots of salsa plus a bit more - An On 2 event for the mambo dancers held in the rooftop club room of the world famous Copacabana, an attractive nightclub in midtown Manhattan - DJ music plus live band on most nights - Pro sound system, wood floor, full bar with drink specials & restaurant menu - see the Facebook Page for this event for names of weekly bands.

TODA LA NOCHE @ STEPPING OUT STUDIOS - Downtown Manhattan - Held on the 1st Saturday of each month - 8:00 PM - 3:00 AM or later - A popular classic On 2 mambo social held in the huge Stepping Out ballroom, tables & lighting like a nightclub, with plenty of room for dancing with lots of partners - A friendly welcoming event with regulars & newcomers attending - With salsa & more by DJ Rudy "The Power Of Love" Acosta and DJ Bongo Loco aka Nick Messina - Presented by fellow salsero & instructor Nick Messina of Bongo Loco - Cash refreshments available

NIEVES LATIN DANCE STUDIO BROOKLYN SALSA SOCIAL - Williamsburg, Brooklyn - 9:00 PM - 12:00 midnight  every Friday - Everyone is invited to this weekly On 2 mambo social held in a fully equipped good size dance studio with pro wooden floor & sound system - Presented by fellow dancer, performer & choreographer Wil Nieves, founder and director of the Nieves Latin Dance Studio who welcomes all mambo dancers

NIEVES LATIN DANCE STUDIO BRONX SALSA SOCIAL - Morrisania-Concourse-South Bronx,, Bronx - Usually held on the 4th Saturday of the month.  See our Main Calendar for exact dates - 9:00 PM - 12:00 midnight - Everyone is invited to this monthly On 2 mambo social held in a fully equipped good size dance studio with pro wooden floor & sound system - Presented by fellow dancer, performer & choreographer Wil Nieves, founder and director of the Nieves Latin Dance Studio who welcomes all mambo dancers

HURACAN SOCIAL @ THE HURACAN DANCE STUDIO - Corona, Queens - 9:00 PM - 3:00 AM - Held on certain dates, usually monthly - See our Main Calendar for exact dates - This is an On 2 mambo social presented by director Jeffrey Taveras & the performers & students of the Huracan Dance Studio & Dance Company - Plenty of classic salsa by DJ "Chuleria" Rich Rodriguez plus a separate bachata room in this professional dance studio with pro sound system & cash beverages available - All are invited - Future 2018 Socials: 

SALSA ON SUNDAY NYC CON SABOR @ DARDO GALLETTO STUDIO - Midtown Manhattan, NYC - Usually held on the 4th Sunday of each month - 6:00 - 10:00 PM - Free admission - A monthly On 2 salsa social for all salseros held in a big air conditioned party venue with a large wooden dance floor & plenty of surrounding chairs for relaxing & socializing, along with a pro sound system & beverage bar - DJ Ray Vega "El Maestro De La Salsa" plays plenty of classic salsa plus a little cha cha, bachata & more - Presented by "Mr. SOS" Edison Hidalgo invites everyone to come join the party

BAILE DEL MAMBO @ STEPPING OUT STUDIOS - Downtown Manhattan, NYC - Every 2nd Friday of the month, 10:15 PM - 3:00 AM - This is a classic On 2 mambo social presented by DJ John John held in a large dance studio with excellent wood ballroom floor & pro sound system - Plenty of classic salsa plus a bit of bachata & merengue by DJ John John & guest DJ TBA - There is a separate bachata room - Complementary 9:30 PM salsa class  bachata class 10:15 PM

SALSA BRUNCH @ ANGEL OF HARLEM - Harlem, Upper West Side Manhattan, NYC - Usually monthly on a Saturday - Dates TBA - See our Main Calendar for exact dates - 12:00 noon to 5:00 PM - A combination of afternoon salsa dancing & a tasty brunch with cocktails held at the very attractive Angel of Harlem located on "Harlem's restaurant row" - With classic salsa & cha cha by DJ Om and presented by our fellow salseros Cesar Vichido & Annika who invite the dancers to come enjoy good food, music & dance - Future 2018 Dates: 

ADDIE-TUDE & JOSIE'S SCHOOL OF DANCE SALSA SOCIAL - Pleasantville, Westchester, NY - Usually held on the 2nd Sunday of each month - 5:00 - 9:00 PM - Intro salsa class 4:30 - 5:00 PM - A classic On 2 salsa social held in a large 2000 square foot studio with pro sound system - Lots of salsa plus a bit of cha cha & bachata by DJ Harry Trinidad - Presented by long time performer & instructor Addie Diaz of the Addie-Tude Dance Company who invites all dancers to come & join the party

LATIN FRIDAYS @ SALSA IN QUEENS - Astoria, Queens, NY - 9:00 PM - 2:00 AM - All are welcome at this monthly mambo social held at the Salsa In Queens dance studio - Founder & director Jean Franco Vergaray invites the tri-state dancers to come for a night of dancing - Plenty of salsa, bachata & more by DJ "Negro Malo" Nelson Cruz on a pro sound system - Located in nearby Astoria - Advance discount admission available

BROOKLYN MUSEUM AFTER WORK SALSA PARTY  - Flatbush, Brooklyn - 6:00 - 9:30 PM - Free Admission - Dance performances by TBA  - Lots of classic salsa plus more Latin music by DJ Ray Suave on a pro sound system + live percussion - Held in the spacious 1st floor front entrance area Glass Pavilion of the museum - Complementary salsa lesson - Held several times per year by Balmir Latin Dance Studio - Complementary dance class 6:00 - 6:45 by instructor Eric Balmir - Upcoming 2018 dates:
